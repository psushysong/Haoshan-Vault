### What the research says

HBR documented it for enterprise AI in March 2026: organizations accumulate successful pilots without converting them into standard practice. A bank built 250 LLM applications; gains stayed localized. The failure mode is not technical. It is the gap between "this works" and "this is now how we operate."

The personal PKM literature reaches the same conclusion from below. The best PKM system is one you will actually use. The failure is not design but maintenance. PKM tools were built assuming users would have time to curate — to highlight, tag, link, review weekly. Modern knowledge workers do not. Organizational decisions (where does this note go? what does this connect to?) accumulate into decision fatigue. The system decays not through misuse but through non-use.

### Why this vault is a case study

The EXPLORE cron generates insight. It finds the right adjacent ideas. It makes actionable proposals. And then they sit.

The COO reads `todo.md` at session start. `todo.md` has never contained any of these proposals. The EXPLORE cron drops notes into `Inbox/_Mid/` — but `_Mid/` is explicitly "everything else worth keeping," not "things that need action." Proposals that land in `_Mid/` are not wrong; they are mis-filed. They needed `_Important/`.

The system designed its own blind spot.

### The actual fix

Not `connections.md`. Not `index.md`. One structural change: the EXPLORE cron should file actionable proposals in `Inbox/_Important/` rather than `_Mid/`. The morning briefing reads `_Important/` and surfaces those items. The COO sees them. The human decides.

Everything else in these notes stays valuable. But proposals that require a decision should never land in `_Mid/`.

### Connection to fallow note

The April 17 note proposed that the vault should model discontinuous activity — gaps as intentional, not as failures. That framing applies here too. The 13-day gap between "good proposal" and "no action" is not a system failure. It is a structural mismatch. The proposal generation loop and the decision loop were never connected.
